TERMS & CONDITIONS

  • Students who will Join any of the National Cyber Security Programs will have to sign a paper before starting of the program to not use their skills for any illegal activity. The NCTP - Program only aims to create Cyber Awareness & to lead Students in Jobs Era. There will be Millions of Cyber Security Jobs in Future as the technology is advancing day by day. According to the U.S. Bureau of Labor and Statistics; as an information security analyst, the median salary is $90,120 per year. But working in the cyber security field is more than just the money.
  • The NCTP - Program is not offering any employment and also not associated with any future employment opportunity. The program aims only to provide training to the students. In case Government Departments nominate NCTP – PROGRAM for Hiring, the Organization will refer the Best Trained Students to the Government which will pass Job Test by NCTP
  • Students at NCTP have to follow all the rules and the regulations otherwise the Organization have the right to take actions against particular candidates
  • According to course completion criteria, trainees will need to secure 70% score to become eligible for Certificate. Each component (Video, Quiz and Hands-on Exercise) carries a certain percentage of the total score of 100 as given below
    • Watching Topic Videos through LMS = 50%
    • Quizzes = 25%
    • Projects = 15%
    • Quarterly Exams = 10%
  • Trainees Will get Certificates after Completion of Course. The tuition fee will be non-refundable once paid
  • The candidate training will be 1 Year long or may the duration increase depend on the content delivered to the students. However the management reserves the right to terminate the training of a candidate at any stage without any reason
  • The selected candidate provided all information should be correct to the best of knowledge of the applicant and if found otherwise at any stage will result in the cancellation of the application
  • Selection / Rejection of a candidate is a purify decision on Qualification and first come first serve basis
  • Any decision by the management regarding training, posting, termination is not challengeable
  • No stipend will be paid to the candidate
  • You have to clear 70% Quizes, Assignments & Exams in each Quarter of 3 Months
